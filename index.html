About Me

Hi there! Im Alex — a passionate web developer from the city of Codeburg. I thrive on turning complex ideas into clean, functional websites that feel effortless to use. Ive always been a bit of an introvert, but I see code as my way of connecting with people. When I’m not experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, you can find me sketching UI designs in my notebook, tinkering with side projects, or sipping tea while brainstorming my next big idea. I love learning new technologies and finding creative ways to solve real-world problems. My ultimate goal is to create digital experiences that are not only visually appealing but also meaningful and accessible to everyone
